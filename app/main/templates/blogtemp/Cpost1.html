Niacinamide serum has earned its star status in the skincare world—and for good reason! Derived from vitamin B3, this powerhouse ingredient is a multitasking marvel that suits every skin type. Whether you're fighting breakouts, tackling dark spots, or just trying to get that lit-from-within glow, niacinamide has got your back (and face).
Let’s dive into what makes niacinamide serum a must-have and how it can truly transform your skincare game.

What is Niacinamide Serum?

Niacinamide serum is a lightweight, water-based formula packed with a concentrated dose of niacinamide—a form of vitamin B3 known for its skin-loving benefits. It’s a topical go-to for improving skin texture, strengthening the barrier, balancing oil production, and calming inflammation.
Think of it as your skin’s all-rounder: it hydrates, soothes, and smooths, all in a few drops. Use it after cleansing and before moisturizing (and don’t forget sunscreen in the morning!).

What’s the Right Percentage of Niacinamide?

  • For beginners or sensitive skin types, 2% to 5% is a great starting point. It delivers benefits without overwhelming your skin.
  • Most effective over-the-counter niacinamide serums come in the 5% to 10% range—ideal for balancing oil, fading dark spots, and refining texture.
  • Concentrations above 10%? Proceed with caution. While some formulations offer 12% or more, they may cause irritation or dryness, especially for sensitive skin.
Stick to 5%–10% for best results—gentle yet effective

How to Use Niacinamide Serum Effectively?

Ready to introduce niacinamide into your routine? Here’s how to get the most out of it:

  • Apply after cleansing and toning, before moisturizing.
  • Use morning and/or night, depending on your routine.
  • Pair it with friendly ingredients like hyaluronic acid, ceramides, or retinol.
  • Always patch-test if you have sensitive skin.

Why Niacinamide Serum Deserves a Spot in Your Routine?

Niacinamide serum is a true multitasker, tackling everything from inflammation and oiliness to dark spots and ageing. Its gentle nature and scientifically proven benefits make it a favourite among skincare experts and enthusiasts alike.
Whether you’re building a simple routine or layering it with other products, niacinamide adapts to your needs. Ready to see healthier, happier skin? Give niacinamide serum a try and discover why it’s earned its reputation as a skincare essential.
