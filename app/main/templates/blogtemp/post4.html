What Is Vitamin C Serum and Why Use It?

So, what is vitamin C serum exactly? Think of it as a superhero for your skin. It’s a potent antioxidant that fights off free radicals—those sneaky culprits that cause dullness and damage.
But it doesn’t stop there. Vitamin C serum benefits include fading dark spots, evening out your skin tone, and boosting collagen to keep your face looking plump and youthful. It’s no surprise it’s a hit with everyone, whether your skin is dry, oily, or sensitive.
The trick? Applying it properly is what unlocks all that magic. Stick with me, and I’ll show you how!

When to Apply Vitamin C Serum in Your Routine?

Okay, let’s talk timing—when’s the best moment to use vitamin C serum in your routine? Most skincare gurus say morning is prime time. Why? It shields your skin from daily stressors like pollution and UV rays. That said, if you’re a night owl, you can use it then too—just know it shines brightest with daytime protection.
Here’s the order: slap it on after cleansing (and toning, if that’s your thing), but before your moisturizer and sunscreen. One heads-up: don’t mix it with retinol at the same time—those two can clash and irritate your skin.
Pro move? Vitamin C in the AM, retinol in the PM. Easy!

How to Apply Vitamin C Serum?

  1. Cleanse: Kick things off by washing your face with a gentle cleanser. This clears away dirt, oil, and makeup so your skin’s ready to soak up the serum. Pat it dry—no rubbing!
  2. Tone (Optional):If you’re into toner, go for it now to balance your skin’s pH. Let it dry completely before the next step.
  3. Apply Serum: Grab your vitamin C serum and squeeze out 2-3 drops onto your fingertips. Gently pat—don’t rub—it onto your face and neck. Patting keeps things gentle and helps it sink in.
  4. Wait: Chill for 1-2 minutes. This gives the serum time to absorb fully before you pile on anything else.
  5. Moisturize:Seal the deal with a lightweight moisturizer. It locks in the serum and keeps your skin happy and hydrated.
  6. Sunscreen (Daytime): If it’s morning, top it off with a broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher. Vitamin C and sunscreen are besties for protecting your glow.

Pro tip:Use clean hands or the dropper to apply. It keeps your serum fresh and free of germs. Follow these apply vitamin C serum steps, and you’re golden!
